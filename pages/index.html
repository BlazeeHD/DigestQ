Features
Everything to master digestion

Six interactive learning activities plus progress tracking and achievements.

🧩

Organ Mock-Up

Drag organ chips onto the correct body slots. Place all 9 organs — including both tract and accessory organs — to earn points. Hints available.

🧬

Interactive Model

Navigate through all 10 digestive organs with real anatomy images, detailed descriptions, and excretory/accessory links for each organ.

🔄

Flow Chart Builder

Scrambled digestive steps — tap them in the correct order to build the Digestive → Excretory flow, then validate your answer instantly.

🔬

Digestive Simulation

Watch digestion unfold across 11 stages. Each organ plays a 4-second video clip in sequence, with full progress tracking.

❓

Knowledge Quiz

15 questions with recall hints before each one. Multiple choice with instant feedback — including image-based identification questions.

✏️

Word Drill

Spell 12 key digestive terms letter-by-letter on an on-screen keyboard. Complete each word to unlock the next — you can't go back.
